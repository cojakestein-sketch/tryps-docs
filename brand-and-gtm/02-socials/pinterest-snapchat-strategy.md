# Tryps Secondary Platform Strategy: Pinterest + Snapchat

**Launch:** May 2, 2026

---

# PINTEREST

## Why It Matters
- 537M monthly users, travel is top 5 search category
- Users come with planning intent (not scrolling to kill time)
- Pins last 3-4 months vs 24 hours on Instagram
- "Group trip ideas" and "trip planning" get millions of monthly searches

## Account Setup
- Business account at business.pinterest.com
- 10 boards: Group Trip Ideas, Weekend Getaways with Friends, Budget Travel Tips, Bachelorette/Bachelor Trips, Summer 2026 Travel, How Marty Plans Your Trip, Packing Lists, Best Airbnbs for Groups, International Group Trips, Trip Planning Humor
- Enable Rich Pins for App Install attribution

## Content Mix
- 40% trip inspiration (destinations, itineraries)
- 25% infographics (budget calculators, packing lists, planning tips)
- 20% product pins (Marty demos, app screenshots, before/after)
- 15% UGC (user trip photos once available)

## Cadence
- Pre-launch: 5-7 pins/day (build library, build search equity)
- Launch week: 10 pins/day
- Ongoing: 3-5 pins/day

## Time Investment
~2 hours/week. Mostly repurposed from TikTok/IG.

## Ads
- Not at launch. Build organic for 30-60 days first.
- Month 3: $500 test budget, promoted pins + app install campaigns
- Expected cost per install: $2-5

# SNAPCHAT

## Why It Matters
- 432M daily users, 75% of US 13-34 year olds
- Spotlight (TikTok competitor) is massively under-saturated
- Same video can get 10x more reach on Spotlight vs TikTok
- Friend groups already coordinate on Snapchat

## Account Setup
- Public Profile enabled
- @tryps or @marty.tryps
- Bio: "Travel Agent for Your Group Chat. Text Marty, he plans everything."

## Content Types
- **Stories (3-7/day):** BTS, quick demos, polls, countdown to launch
- **Spotlight (3-5/week):** Repurposed TikToks without watermark. 15-30 sec performs best.
- **AR Lens (free to create):** "Planned by Marty" photo frame filter

## Repurposing from TikTok
- Export TikTok without watermark, upload to Spotlight
- Different caption text (Spotlight has its own algorithm)
- 15-30 min/week to batch repost

## Ads (launch month, $1-2K)
- 70% Snap Ads (app installs, $2-6 per install)
- 30% Story Ads (awareness)
- Target: US, 18-30, travel interests

## Time Investment
~1 hour/week. Mostly repurposed.

---

## Priority
Pinterest > Snapchat for long-term (search intent). Snapchat > Pinterest for short-term virality (Spotlight). Both are low effort since content is repurposed from primary platforms.
