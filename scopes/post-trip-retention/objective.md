## What

What happens after the trip — reviews, memories, photos, and mechanisms that keep people coming back. Post-trip review flow, photo gallery, and rewards program (Tryps Miles).

## Why

The trip lifecycle doesn't end at the airport. Post-trip review creates the emotional moment that drives sharing and repeat usage. Without retention mechanisms, every trip is a one-shot experience.
