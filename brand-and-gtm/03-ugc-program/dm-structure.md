# Tryps UGC DM Structure

## How Sean Reaches Out to 100 Creators

### DM 1 — Open the door
"hey, love your page. quick question for you"

### DM 2 — After they reply, get to the point
"we're launching a travel app on May 2 and I want to hire you to post about it. $100 for one video. Interested?"

### DM 3 — After they say yes, explain
"bet. so the app is called Tryps. You can add a group travel agent to your iMessage group chat and the travel agent plans your whole trip through the group chat. All I need is a short video talking about it in your style. I'll send you early access today."

### If no reply to DM 1
One bump 3 days later: "hey just bumping this!" No third attempt.

---

## Contest Structure
- 100 creators each make a video talking about Tryps in their style
- Formats: hot takes, "I found something about to blow up," rants about being the trip planner
- Top 5 most-viewed videos get their trip paid for by Tryps
- Budget option A: $50/creator upfront ($5K) + $3K trip prizes = $8K
- Budget option B: No upfront, full $8K into trip prizes

## How to Find Creators
- Search TikTok/IG for the behavior: "planning a group trip," "booking with friends," "group chat trip"
- Watch their content, check engagement rate (3%+ minimum)
- Build Google Sheet with handle, followers, tier, engagement, notes on WHY they made the list
- Pre-engage before DMing: follow, like 2-3 posts, leave one real comment, wait 2-3 days, then DM
- Batch 15-20 DMs per day to avoid getting flagged
- Time block: 1 hour morning, 1 hour evening

## How to Explain Tryps
"You can add a group travel agent to your iMessage group chat and the travel agent plans your whole trip through the group chat."
