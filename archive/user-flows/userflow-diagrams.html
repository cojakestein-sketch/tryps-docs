Voting Mechanics

  • Who votes: Trip members only (RLS enforced)
  • Deadline: 24h / 48h / 1 week (configurable by owner). NOT auto-enforced — owner must "Lock it in"
  • Tie-breaking: Owner picks any option (human tiebreaker)
  • Owner can unlock finalized choices to reopen voting
  • Destination + date votes run simultaneously with a single deadline
  • Group Can Suggest toggle: allows members to propose new options during voting
  • Max Winners: 1 / Top 2 / Top 3 (for multi-destination trips from voting)

Sub-Components to Design

  • Destination chip autocomplete (matching engine suggests from trip name)
  • Destination mode toggle ("Visit all" vs "Let group decide") — appears at 2+ chips
  • Voting settings panel (deadline, max winners, group can suggest)
  • Date mode selector (exact vs flexible/voting)
  • Weekend chip selector (smart suggestions from timeframe input)
  • Draggable timeline (allocate days per city for multi-city + exact dates)
  • Card preview (live-updating with font, theme, destinations, dates)
  • Cover image picker (auto / upload / stock)
  • Vibe quiz (6 dimension sliders)
  • Success modal with share options
