## What

Creating, managing, and visualizing trips — plus expense tracking. This is the foundation everything else sits on. Trip creation, editing, multi-city, phases, covers, links, calendar, home tab, discover, and all expense flows.

## Why

This is the app skeleton. Every other scope builds on top of a working trip experience. If core flows don't work, nothing else matters.

- All 19 core flows QA validated
- Trip card customization shipped
- All expense flows working end-to-end
