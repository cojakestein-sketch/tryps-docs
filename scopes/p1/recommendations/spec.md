## Intent

The recommendation engine that makes Tryps smart. Five sub-scopes cover different recommendation types, plus the core algorithm that ties them together. Recommendations are powered by Travel DNA, trip context, and group preferences.

## Sub-Scopes

### A. Activity Recommendations
Based on Travel DNA profiles + destination. "Your group loves adventure — here are the top-rated activities in Tulum."

### B. Trip Recommendations
Based on group DNA overlap. "3 of you are beach people — here are trips your group would love."

### C. Flight Recommendations
Based on arrival time and trip days. "If your trip starts Friday, here are the best Thursday night flights."

### D. Accommodation Recommendations
Based on stay preferences from DNA. "Your group prefers Airbnbs over hotels — here are the top-rated options."

### E. Discovery Engine
"Discover other places to recommend things to users" — location-based discovery, trending destinations, seasonal recommendations.

### Core Algorithm
Scoring, ranking, and personalization engine that powers all sub-scopes.
