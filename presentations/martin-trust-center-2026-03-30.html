Layer 1
iMessage
Where conversations happen. Zero behavior change.

Competitive Differential

Our Right to Win

First to market with native polling in iMessage

We put an agent where people already coordinate — their group chat. If we can make it effortless to communicate preferences, we win in this era of human-shared, agent-executed decisions.

Data Moat: Shared Trip Memory

No one else holds the full record of what a group decided, preferred, and experienced together. More trips = smarter agent = more return usage.

Three Layers, One Product

Anyone can build a travel app. Nobody integrates iMessage + native mobile + autonomous booking into one seamless product.

The Group Traveler (20s–30s)

People who believe travel is innate. Bachelor/bachelorette parties, friend groups, reunions. Willing to spend — looking for someone to handle it.

The Grill: Agent as Facilitator

Our agent runs on schedule, steering the group toward completion — polling, guiding, nudging — until the trip is booked. Facilitation, not dictation.
