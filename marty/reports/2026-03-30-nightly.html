Tryps runs on Claude. Our entire agent-intelligence scope (61 SCs), iMessage agent, and dev workflow (Claude Code) depend on Anthropic's stack. A "step change" model means: (1) our AI trip Q&A and recommendation engine could get dramatically better overnight, (2) Claude Code productivity gains for the team, and (3) we need to watch pricing — Anthropic is already adjusting session limits during peak hours (Forbes, March 26).   • Competitive moat — Being deeply integrated with the fastest-growing AI platform is a good position. The "call Tryps, ask anything, get a great answer" thesis gets stronger as models improve.
