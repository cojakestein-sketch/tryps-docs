# The Problem

Planning a group trip is broken.

- **47 texts** to pick a restaurant. Nobody books it.
- The "planner friend" does all the work. Everyone else ghosts.
- Details are scattered across iMessage, Google Docs, Venmo, and someone's Notes app
- By the time you land, half the group doesn't know the plan

> The group chat is where trips get planned. But nothing that happens there turns into action.
