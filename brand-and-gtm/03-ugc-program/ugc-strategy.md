# Tryps UGC Targeting & Outreach Strategy

## Executive Summary

Tryps launches May 2, 2026. The UGC program is designed to create a wave of authentic creator content during launch week that makes Tryps feel like it's everywhere.

**The plan:** Engage 80 creators across TikTok and Instagram to post about Tryps. Two tiers: mid-tier (50K-500K) for credibility, micro (10K-50K) for volume. Total budget: $8,000 (separate from Sean's contract). Micro creators get $100 each. Mid-tier creators get $200-400 each.

**Two-phase approach:**
1. **Launch contest** — "Go on Your Dream Trip." Creators repost/share to enter. Low effort, high volume. The prize: Tryps pays for the winner's dream trip, destination of their choice.
2. **Paid content** — Creators make original GRWM-style videos showing how they plan a trip using Tryps in their group chat. Real demos, real friend groups, real trips.

**DM outreach** comes from @tryps by default. Sean manages all correspondence and personalizes each message. Jake sends personally to warm contacts only.

**The list:** Molly, Ana, Gianna, and Cash build the 300-person prospect list using the creator criteria doc. Sean and Jake have identified 13 reference creators as benchmarks. The team finds 287 more.

**Timeline:** Strategy delivered by April 2 (M1). List built and outreach starts week of April 7. All 80 creators engaged by May 2 (M2). Content goes live launch week.

## 1. Targeting Strategy

### Who We're Reaching

The Tryps core user is a 22-28 year old post-grad. Single, social, active. The person in the group chat who actually makes plans happen. Goes on 1-2 group trips a year. iPhone user. Watches travel and lifestyle content on TikTok and Instagram. Wears Patagonia, not Gucci. Performative in a good way — they want people to see where they are and what they're doing.

**Trip types:** Weekend trips, international trips, road trips, festival groups.

### Who We're Targeting as Creators

**Creator segments (ranked by priority):**

1. **Lifestyle / GRWM / going-out creators** — Show their life, their audience wants to live like them. Highest priority.
2. **Travel creators** — Direct product overlap. Strong fit.
3. **Friend group YouTubers** — David Dobrik model. Group energy IS the Tryps vibe. YouTube presence is a strong signal for audience loyalty.
4. **Solo creators planning trips** — The organizer archetype. They'd demo Tryps as the tool that makes group planning easy.
5. **Festival / event creators** — Coachella model. Longer-term play, worth including in the list now.

### Two Tiers

| Tier | Follower Range | Role | Payment | Volume Target |
|------|---------------|------|---------|---------------|
| Mid | 50K - 500K | Credibility. Makes the app feel legitimate. | $200-400 each | 10-15 creators |
| Micro | 10K - 50K | Volume. Creates the wave of content. | $100 each | 65-70 creators |

**Minimum follower count: 10,000.** Below 10K is not considered for this campaign.

**Total: 80 creators. Budget: $8,000.**

### Platforms

TikTok and Instagram, treated 50/50 as equal priority. YouTube is a secondary platform, specifically valuable for friend-group ensemble content and travel vlogs. X and YouTube Shorts are not a focus for creator sourcing.

### The 30-Second Profile Check

When evaluating a creator, look for:

**Yes signals:** Weekly high-quality Reels (not daily spam). Curated lifestyle grid. Consistent engagement across entire feed. Real conversations in comments. Normal person energy. Traveling, social, active. YouTube channel is a bonus.

**No signals:** Inconsistent engagement (viral spikes + dead posts). Salesy energy. Bought followers. Daily content spam. Luxury-only travel. No Stories in 2+ weeks.

**Engagement threshold:** Average likes across last 10 posts divided by follower count. Above 3% is good. Below 2% is a skip.

Full criteria in `creator-criteria.md`.

## 2. Outreach Strategy

### Who Sends the DMs

| Scenario | Sender | Account |
|----------|--------|---------|
| Default (all outreach) | Sean | @tryps brand account |
| Low response rate on specific target | Whoever has more mutuals | Sean's personal or Jake's personal |
| Warm contacts (people Jake knows) | Jake | Jake's personal |

**Mutuals are the deciding factor.** When choosing between accounts, check who has more shared connections with the target. That's what gets DMs opened.

### The DM Flow

Short, direct, 3-step approach. Sean personalizes each message based on the creator's content.

**DM 1 — Open the door**

> hey, love your page. quick question for you

**DM 2 — After they reply, pitch**

> we're launching a travel app on May 2 and I want to hire you to post about it. $100 for one video. Interested?

(Adjust dollar amount for mid-tier targets.)

**DM 3 — After they say yes, explain**

> bet. so the app is called Tryps. You can add a group travel agent to your iMessage group chat and the travel agent plans your whole trip through the group chat. All I need is a short video talking about it in your style. I'll send you early access today.

**If no reply to DM 1:** One bump 3 days later. No third attempt.

**Key rules:**
- Sean curates each response to the specific creator. This is not copy-paste mass outreach.
- Batch 15-20 DMs per day max to avoid getting flagged by Instagram.
- Pre-engage before DMing: follow, like 2-3 posts, leave one real comment, wait 2-3 days, then DM.
- Best times to DM: Tuesday through Thursday, 10am-1pm or 7-9pm in their timezone.

### Tracking

Google Sheet with: Creator name, handle, platform, followers, tier, engagement rate, DM sent date, response, status, notes on why they made the list.

## 3. Offer Structure & Deliverables

### Phase 1: Launch Contest — "Go on Your Dream Trip"

The first activation is a repost contest designed for maximum reach with minimum creator effort.

**Mechanic:**
- Creators repost/share a @tryps contest post to enter
- Prize: **"Where do you want to go? We'll send you there."** Tryps pays for the winner's dream trip. Their choice of destination. Potentially first-class flight.
- Quality bar enforced. Low-effort entries are disqualified.
- Smaller incentive layer possible: post on your Story, get a discount from a Tryps partner brand.

**Why this works:** The prize IS the product (travel). Creators are self-motivated to participate because the prize is genuinely exciting. You get potentially hundreds of entries for the cost of 1-2 trips. The ambition is to make this a cultural moment, not just a marketing campaign.

### Phase 2: Paid Original Content

Once creators are engaged, the paid deliverable is original content.

**What creators make:** A GRWM-style video showing how they plan a trip with their friends using Tryps in the group chat. It should feel like their normal content with Tryps naturally integrated.

**Per tier:**
- **Micro ($100):** 1 Reel or 1 TikTok. Their choice of platform. Tag @tryps.
- **Mid ($200-400):** 1 Reel + 1 TikTok + Story set. Tag @tryps. More money, more content.

**Content direction:**
- Show the actual app in use (screen recording of iMessage, Tryps in the group chat)
- Include real friends reacting
- Talk about a real trip they're planning
- Use their normal editing style
- Should feel like their content, not an ad

**Posting window:** May 2-9, 2026 (launch week). Ideal: May 2-4 for launch day momentum. Drafts reviewed 48 hours before posting.

**Payment:** Venmo, PayPal, or bank transfer within 7 days of content going live.

**Usage rights:** Tryps can repost on @tryps channels and potentially use in paid ads with credit. If creator opts out of paid ad usage, content stays organic only.

**FTC disclosure:** All creators include "Partnered with @tryps" or "#ad" in caption. Required, non-negotiable.

## 5. Brand Ambassador Program

### Overview

Separate from the 80-creator launch blitz, Tryps will run an exclusive Brand Ambassador Program. This is the premium tier — a small number of creators who represent the brand on an ongoing basis, not a one-off campaign.

**Structure:** 4 ambassadors at a time. Each ambassador produces 1 Instagram collab post per month, staggered so one goes live each week. 3-month commitment per ambassador cycle.

**How it's different from the launch creators:**

| | Launch Creators (80) | Brand Ambassadors (4) |
|---|---|---|
| Purpose | Volume — make Tryps feel everywhere | Brand — define what Tryps looks and feels like |
| Commitment | One-time (repost + optional video) | 3-month retainer, 1 collab post/month |
| Content type | Their style, light integration | Satirical/personality-driven content with Tryps-branded thumbnails |
| Compensation | $50-500 one-time | $1,000-3,000/month retainer + perks |
| Scouting | Broad outreach, 300-person list | Hand-picked for brand fit, aesthetic, and cultural influence |

### Ambassador Profile

The ideal Tryps ambassador has a cult following, not just a follower count. They set the tone for their audience. Reference creators: @fibula, @discolines, @brookeaverick, @passthatpuss.

**Selection criteria beyond follower count:**
- Engagement rate above 3% (Instagram) or 5% (TikTok)
- Real conversations in their comments — followers asking questions, tagging friends
- Content that already includes travel, friend groups, or social lifestyle without being paid to post it
- High production value on thumbnails and editing, but authentic and funny in the video itself
- Audience demographics match Tryps core user (18-30, US-based)
- Has done 1-3 brand partnerships before but isn't oversaturated

### Content Format

Each ambassador creates 1 collab post per month (appears on both the ambassador's grid and @tryps). The content is a satirical, personality-driven video explaining how Tryps has changed the way they plan trips — similar in tone to [this example](https://www.instagram.com/p/DWXWAP9Dl24/).

**Key requirements:**
- The creator chooses the concept. Sean provides concept ideas and direction, but the creator owns the creative.
- Each video thumbnail has a Tryps-branded aesthetic that looks good on both the creator's feed and the @tryps grid.
- No screen recordings or screenshots of the app. If showing the product, it's filmed by a friend while the creator holds their phone and walks through it. Must feel like a friend filming a friend.

**Content calendar (monthly, staggered):**

| Week | Ambassador |
|------|-----------|
| Week 1 | Ambassador A posts collab |
| Week 2 | Ambassador B posts collab |
| Week 3 | Ambassador C posts collab |
| Week 4 | Ambassador D posts collab |

This gives @tryps one piece of premium branded content per week, every week, for the duration of the program.

### Compensation

**Monthly retainer:** $1,000-3,000/month depending on the creator's reach and engagement. Quality over quantity — it's better to pay more for 4 creators with real cultural influence than to spread the budget across 20 mediocre ones.

**Milestone bonus:** $500 bonus when an ambassador hits a sign-up milestone through their unique referral link. Clean, predictable, no per-unit tracking needed.

**Perks:**
- Merch and care packages
- Paid trips — Tryps funds a trip for the ambassador and their friends (also generates content)
- Event invites and content retreats
- Early access to new features
- "Founding Ambassador" status

**Quarterly budget (4 ambassadors):**
- Retainers: $12,000-36,000/quarter
- Milestone bonuses: up to $2,000/quarter (capped)
- Trips/perks: variable
- Total: ~$15,000-40,000/quarter depending on tier

### Contract & Operations

**Contract:** 3-month commitment. Includes content deliverables, usage rights (Tryps gets perpetual license to repurpose for organic and paid), category exclusivity (no competing travel apps), FTC disclosure requirements, 2 rounds of revisions per content piece, and termination clause.

**Legal:** Use a template (Creators Legal, ~$100-200) and have a lawyer review once ($500-800). Reuse for all ambassadors.

**Onboarding (per ambassador):**
1. Brand packet — visual guidelines, messaging pillars, content examples, what not to say
2. 30-minute kickoff call — walk through brand, brainstorm content angles for their audience
3. Shared asset folder — logos, product screenshots, demo videos, their unique referral link
4. Product access — ambassador must actually use Tryps in a real group chat

**Content approval workflow:**
- Ambassador submits content plan at start of month (bullet points per post)
- Draft submitted 5 business days before post date
- Sean reviews within 48 hours
- Ambassador has 48 hours for revisions (2 rounds max)
- Final approval within 24 hours

**Payment:** Monthly invoices, paid via ACH or PayPal. W-9 collected before first payment. 1099-NEC issued at year-end.

**Tracking:** Google Sheet with columns for ambassador, month, retainer, bonus, total, invoice status, payment date. Referral links via Dub.co or Short.io.

**Time commitment for Sean:** ~5-8 hours/week across all 4 ambassadors.

### Affiliate Tracking

Each ambassador gets a unique referral link (e.g., tryps.co/ref/ambassadorname) via Dub.co or Short.io. Tracks clicks and sign-ups. Milestone bonuses paid based on monthly sign-up totals — no per-unit math, just "hit 100 sign-ups, get $500."

Full per-sign-up affiliate payouts deferred until Tryps monetizes. Ambassadors are told upfront: "When Tryps has revenue, you'll be first in line for rev share."

Ambassadors are scouted separately from the 300-person launch list. Sean identifies and approaches candidates starting in Week 3 of the launch campaign (mid-May), with the first ambassador cycle beginning June 2026.
