## What

First-run experience, tooltips, and guided moments that make Tryps instantly obvious. The first 60-second iMessage experience, app download CTA from group chat, and in-app teaching moments.

## Why

Two things must click instantly: (1) there's an app behind the agent, and (2) just communicate your vibe and Tryps handles the rest. If the first experience is confusing, users drop before they ever see the value.

- Tooltips guide feature discovery without being annoying
- New trip members understand the product within seconds
