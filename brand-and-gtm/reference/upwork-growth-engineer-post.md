## Upwork Post: GTM Growth Engineer

**Title:** AI-Native Growth Engineer for Consumer App Launch (May 2)

**Category:** Marketing > Growth Marketing

---

**Description:**

We're Tryps — a group travel planning app launching May 2, 2026. You add a travel agent to your iMessage group chat, text it your trip idea, and it plans the whole thing. Think Partiful but for travel.

We're building a 3-person brand and go-to-market team under our creative director. We need a flexible, AI-native growth engineer who can execute across our entire GTM operation — not a specialist in one channel.

**What this role looks like:**

You're the person Sean (our creative director) and Jake (founder) point at whatever the GTM org needs that week. One week you're writing App Store copy and running keyword tests. The next you're managing $5K in Meta ad spend and flagging which UGC clips are winning. The week after that you're writing SEO landing pages or prepping our Product Hunt launch.

You use AI tools (Claude, ChatGPT, CapCut, Canva, Recharm) as a multiplier — not as a crutch. You're technical enough to use Claude Code for scripts and workflows, marketing-savvy enough to write copy that converts, and fast enough to produce volume. The goal is 100+ distributed content pieces per month across social, ads, search, App Store, and email.

**Week-to-week scope includes:**

- Social content production — screen recordings, carousels, clip edits using templates and AI tools. Schedule across 8 platforms.
- Meta ads — manage $5K/month spend, generate 50+ ad variants from UGC creator content using Recharm, A/B test, optimize daily.
- App Store optimization — write and test metadata, run Apple PPO screenshot tests, monitor conversion.
- SEO/AEO — write landing pages and blog posts targeting high-intent keywords, implement structured data for AI search engines.
- UGC operational support — run creators through verification tools, track deliverables, manage payment logistics. (Our creative director owns relationships.)
- Email — write launch blast and onboarding drip sequences.
- Product Hunt — prep listing and coordinate launch day.
- Press support — build journalist list, draft pitches, create press kit.
- Whatever else needs doing that week.

**You are NOT:**

- A strategist. The strategy is set. We need execution.
- A specialist. If you can only do one thing (just Meta ads, just SEO), this isn't the right fit.
- A designer. You use AI tools and templates, not Figma from scratch.
- Managing influencer relationships. Our creative director handles that.

**What we're looking for:**

- You've run Meta ads at a startup ($5-20K/month range is fine — not looking for agency experience at $500K/month).
- You've written App Store copy, landing pages, or marketing emails before.
- You use AI tools daily (Claude, ChatGPT, Midjourney, CapCut, etc.) and can point to specific workflows where AI made you faster.
- You can use Claude Code or similar tools to build simple scripts and workflows when needed.
- When asked to do something you've never done, your answer is "give me 2 hours" not "that's not my job."
- Startup speed. We ship fast, iterate faster.

**Nice to have:**

- Experience with App Store optimization (ASO)
- TikTok Spark Ads experience
- SEO/content marketing background
- Previous consumer app launch experience
- Familiar with n8n, Make, or similar automation tools

**Logistics:**

- 20-30 hours/week contractor
- Slack access, 2-3x weekly meetings with creative director and founder
- Start immediately — we launch May 2
- $30-50/hour depending on experience

**To apply:**

Tell me about the last time you used AI tools to produce marketing output 10x faster than the traditional way. Be specific — what tools, what output, how much time saved. That's the kind of work this role is.
