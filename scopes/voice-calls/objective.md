## What

An AI voice concierge on the Tryps phone number. Call in, talk to the same agent brain that powers iMessage and the app. Create trips, get recommendations, ask questions — entirely by voice. No app required.

## Why

Voice is the most natural way to plan travel. The Tryps number already handles text. Adding voice makes it a real travel agency — one number, every channel. Anyone can call and plan a trip without downloading anything.

## Key Concepts

- **One Brain, Every Channel:** Same agent backend as iMessage and app. Same tools, same trip state, same memory.
- **Voice is Standalone:** New users onboard entirely by voice — no app required.
- **Full State Sync:** Voice call outcomes (trips created, searches run, recs given) sync to the trip for all members.
- **Fast-First Tone:** Efficient, short answers, human-sounding but never chatty. Max ~25 sec per speaking turn.

## Success Looks Like

A stranger calls the Tryps number, talks to the agent for 90 seconds, and has a trip created with a link texted to them. An existing user calls about their Barcelona trip and gets restaurant recommendations in 30 seconds. Everything from the call shows up in the app for other trip members.
