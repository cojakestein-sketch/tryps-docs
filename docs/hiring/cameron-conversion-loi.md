# Letter of Intent — Full-Time Head of Product

**Date:** ____________, 2026

**To:** Cameron ____________

**From:** Jake Stein, Founder & CEO, Tryps

---

Cameron,

This letter describes our shared intent for what a full-time role at Tryps would look like if our consulting engagement goes well. **This letter is non-binding** — it outlines the direction we're both working toward, not a commitment by either side.

---

## The Engagement So Far

You're joining Tryps as a part-time consultant (Head of Product) under a separate Consulting Services Agreement dated ____________, 2026. That agreement covers the period through May 2, 2026, with five defined milestones and $10,000 in milestone-based compensation.

This letter looks ahead to what happens if you deliver on those milestones and we both want to continue.

---

## What Full-Time Would Look Like

If the consulting engagement is successful and both parties agree to proceed, Company intends to extend a full-time employment offer with the following anticipated terms:

### Title

**Head of Product**

### Equity

An equity grant in the anticipated range of **1.0% – 1.5%** of fully diluted shares, subject to:
- Board approval
- Completion of a 409A valuation
- Standard stock option agreement terms

**Vesting:** 4-year vesting schedule with a 1-year cliff, vesting monthly thereafter. Company will consider crediting 2-3 months of the consulting engagement period toward the cliff, recognizing your early contribution.

**Vehicle:** Incentive Stock Options (ISOs) if eligible; Non-Qualified Stock Options (NSOs) otherwise.

### Salary

Salary will be determined at the time of the offer based on the Company's fundraising status:

| Scenario | Anticipated Range |
|----------|------------------|
| Post $1-2M seed raise | $110,000 – $130,000 |
| Post $3M+ seed raise | $130,000 – $150,000 |

These ranges reflect the standard trade-off between cash and equity at seed-stage startups.

### Benefits

Standard benefits consistent with other senior employees at the time of hire (details TBD based on what the company offers at that stage).

### Acceleration

Double-trigger acceleration only: equity accelerates if (1) the Company undergoes a change of control AND (2) Consultant is terminated without cause within 12 months of the event.

---

## Conversion Timeline

- **May 2, 2026:** End of consulting engagement. Milestone assessment.
- **By May 16, 2026:** Both parties communicate conversion decision.
- **If converting:** Full-time offer letter issued within 14 days of mutual decision. Cameron provides notice at current employer. Transition period begins.
- **If not converting:** Clean exit. Cameron retains all consulting fees earned. No further obligations on either side.

---

## What This Letter Is and Isn't

**This IS:**
- A good-faith statement of intent from both sides
- A framework for what we're both working toward
- A signal that Tryps takes this relationship seriously

**This is NOT:**
- A binding contract or employment offer
- A guarantee of employment, equity, or compensation
- An obligation on either party to proceed with conversion

The specific terms of any full-time offer — including exact equity percentage, salary, and benefits — will be negotiated and documented in a separate employment agreement at the time of conversion.

---

## The Bigger Picture

I'm building Tryps to be the way people plan trips with their friends. You'd be the person who makes sure the product actually delivers on that promise. The consulting period is about us both figuring out if this is the right fit — and if it is, I want to make sure you're set up to own this role for real.

I'm heading to MIT in fall 2026. If you're the right person, you'll be carrying the product function with real authority while I'm in school. That matters, and the equity and role should reflect it.

Let's prove it works in the next month, then build something great.
