## What

Visual identity, design tokens, and Figma assets — building the world. The brand is aligned in the app (Plus Jakarta Sans, #D9071C, warm palette). A few outstanding screens need polish. Brand book in Figma needs to be built.

## Why

The design system is the foundation for every screen in the app. It also blocks other scopes — new UI screens depend on brand alignment being complete. The brand book is how we communicate the visual identity to designers and stakeholders.

## Success Looks Like

- All outstanding screens polished and aligned to brand
- Brand book in Figma (30-40 screens) complete
- Design tokens consistent across tryps.pen, theme.ts, and Figma
