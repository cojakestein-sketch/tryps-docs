# The Honest Assessment

Everybody can make a viral mobile app. We've seen it:

- **Partiful** did it for parties
- **Strava** did it for running
- **Beli** did it for restaurants

Simple UI, friends share it, captures data. We do it for travel.

**But we don't think the mobile app alone gives us any right to win.** Other players probably have a better right to win on mobile alone.

# Where We Actually Win

Nobody has taken the mobile app and **integrated these two layers on top and beneath it.**

## iMessage: Where the conversation already lives
- Partnered with Linq for best-in-class iMessage infrastructure
- **Nobody** can do native group polling in iMessage today
- We are the first beta tester of Linq's polling feature
- When it deploys, we are the **only platform** where people vote on trip decisions in iMessage

## Agent Layer: AI that executes the plan
- X402 protocols enable our agent to search, book, and coordinate
- Triggered directly from the iMessage conversation
- No context switching. No app hopping. The agent just does it.

---

# The Moat is the Stack

1. **iMessage** — where the conversation already lives (no behavior change)
2. **Mobile App** — for people who want visibility and control
3. **Agent Layer** — AI that executes the plan end-to-end

Nobody else has all three. That's the right to win.
