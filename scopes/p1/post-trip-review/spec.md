## Intent

The post-trip experience that brings groups back to Tryps. Centered around a 60-second montage video that surfaces once a year ("on this day"), a top-3 activities vote, and trip sentiment/rating. The goal is to create a memorable capstone that drives word-of-mouth and re-engagement.

## Key Areas

- 60-second montage video (annual auto-generated recap)
- Top 3 activities selection (group votes post-trip)
- Post-trip sentiment review & trip rating
- Yearly "on this day" memories (push notification + deeplink)
- Post-trip photo gallery & highlights reel
