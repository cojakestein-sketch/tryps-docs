## Upwork Post: Content Creator — Video, SEO, Social (App Launch)

**Title:** Content Creator — Video, SEO, Social (App Launch)

**Category:** Social Media Marketing

**Skills to tag:** Social Media Content Creation, Video Editing, SEO Writing, Content Marketing, TikTok, Instagram, Short-Form Video, Canva, CapCut, Figma

**Hours:** 30-40 hrs/week

**Rate:** $15-30/hr (depending on location and experience)

**Experience Level:** Intermediate

---

**Description:**

Tryps is a group travel planning app launching May 2, 2026. Think Partiful but for trips — it replaces the scattered mess of iMessage threads + Google Docs + Splitwise with one place to plan, decide, book, and split costs with your friends.

We need a content creator who can produce polished video, write for search engines (and AI engines), and run our social media presence. This is a generalist role at a small startup, not a specialist gig. You'll touch everything content-related.

**What you'll do every week:**

- **Produce short-form video (40% of your time):** Screen-record the app and turn raw footage into polished 15-second product demo clips with text overlays, transitions, and branded elements. Think @claudeai on X or Partiful's App Store video. Also produce Reels, TikToks, and YouTube Shorts from a mix of app footage, UGC, and original content.

- **Write for SEO and AEO (30%):** Blog posts, landing page copy, comparison articles ("Tryps vs Splitwise vs Google Docs for group trips"), and App Store descriptions. You should also understand how to write content that gets cited by AI assistants like ChatGPT and Perplexity when someone asks "what's the best group trip planning app?"

- **Manage and publish across social platforms (20%):** Schedule and post across TikTok, Instagram, and X (primary), plus YouTube Shorts, Facebook, LinkedIn, Snapchat, and Pinterest (secondary). Target is 15 posts/week across the 3 primary platforms. You own the content calendar.

- **Whatever else the GTM org needs that week (10%):** Create carousels and social graphics. Design ad creatives. Write email copy. Build a one-pager. Edit a testimonial video. This role flexes week to week.

**Eventually (not day 1):**

- Run and optimize $5K/month Meta ad campaigns
- A/B test ad variants from UGC and product footage
- Help shape referral and viral loop content

**The team:**

You'll report to the founder (Jake). A creative director (Sean) reviews and approves all outgoing content. The dev team ships fast, so you'll always have new features to showcase. This is a 5-person startup, not a marketing department. You'll have real ownership.

**Required:**

- Portfolio with at least 3 examples of short-form product or app demo videos you edited (screen recordings with overlays, not just talking head clips)
- Demonstrated SEO writing samples — blog posts or articles that rank or were written with keyword intent
- Experience managing social media accounts with a content calendar (not just posting for yourself)
- Proficiency in video editing tools (CapCut, Premiere Pro, Final Cut, or similar)
- Proficiency in design tools (Canva, Figma, or similar) for static graphics
- Strong written English — you can write a blog post and a TikTok caption and they both sound natural

**Nice to have:**

- Experience with consumer app or travel industry content
- Understanding of App Store Optimization (ASO)
- Familiarity with AI answer engine optimization (AEO) — structuring content so AI assistants cite it
- Experience running Meta or TikTok ads
- Motion graphics or animation skills

**Audition task (required with your proposal):**

Pick any mobile app you like. Screen-record yourself using it for 30-60 seconds of raw footage. Then produce a polished 15-second demo clip with text overlays, transitions, and at least one branded element (logo, color scheme, or watermark). Attach the final clip to your proposal. This is the single most important thing we'll evaluate.

**To apply, include all three:**

1. Link your portfolio or 3 best short-form video samples. At least one must be a product/app screen recording with editing.

2. Send a link to one piece of SEO content you wrote (blog post, comparison article, landing page). Tell us what keyword you targeted and what it ranked for (or what the intent was).

3. What's your weekly availability for the next 3 months? Are you taking on other clients simultaneously?
