# Launch Video Concept: "However You Get To Us"

> Status: Raw concept — needs treatment pass with Sean
> Created: 2026-03-26
> Author: Jake

## Core Idea

**One visual rule:** Everything is neutral — white rooms, neutral wardrobe, muted tones. The ONLY color in each frame is Tryps Red (#D9071C), and it's always the device through which that person is interacting with Tryps.

**One message:** However you get to us, we come to you. Every generation, every device, every comfort level with technology — Tryps meets you where you are.

## Characters (Draft)

Each character represents a different generation and interaction style:

| Character | Generation | Device (Red) | Interaction Style |
|-----------|-----------|--------------|-------------------|
| The Coder | Gen Z (male) | Red laptop | Power user — has his trip built in 5 seconds, hacking away |
| The Texter | Gen Z (female) | Red phone case | Texting naturally, effortless, group chat native |
| The Grateful Mom | Millennial | TBD (red iPad? red case?) | Relieved, thankful — "please say thank you for this" energy |
| The Grandma | Boomer+ | Big old red phone (landline/rotary?) | Super deliberate, twiddly-dally, calling in. Calls Jake(?). |
| The Quirky Uncle | Gen X? | Red Blackberry or flip phone | Somehow makes it work on ancient tech |

## Visual Treatment

- **Setting:** All-white rooms. Clean, minimal, almost commercial-grade neutral.
- **Wardrobe:** Everyone in neutral colors — whites, grays, creams, tans.
- **Color rule:** Tryps Red is the ONLY saturated color in every shot. It pops because everything else is desaturated.
- **The device IS the brand.** No logo cards, no UI demos needed — the red device is the logo.

## Tone

- Not a product demo. Not a feature walkthrough.
- It's a brand film. "Who is Tryps for? Everyone."
- Warm, a little funny (grandma's giant red phone, uncle's Blackberry), but never condescending.
- Each character is competent in their own way — they're all getting the trip done.

## Thematic Thread

The tagline energy is: **"We come to you."**

Tryps isn't asking you to download an app, learn a new workflow, or change how you communicate. It meets every person through the channel they already use. The video proves this by showing wildly different people, wildly different devices, all arriving at the same result.
