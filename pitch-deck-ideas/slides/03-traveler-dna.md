# We Don't Just Know What You Like. We Know Why You Travel.

### Three layers of traveler understanding

| Layer | What It Captures | How Tryps Gets It |
|-------|-----------------|-------------------|
| **Traits** (Trip Vibe) | Beach vs. city, chill vs. packed | A/B vibe quiz at trip creation |
| **Adaptations** (Trip Intent) | What you want from *this* trip | One open-ended question + NLP |
| **Narrative** (Travel DNA) | Why you travel — your evolving story | Cross-trip pattern recognition |

> Based on Dan McAdams' (Northwestern) three-level personality framework. No travel app thinks about personality at this depth.
