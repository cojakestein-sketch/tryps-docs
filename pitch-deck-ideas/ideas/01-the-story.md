# The Story

**One-liner:** Tryps is the travel agent in your iMessage group chat.

It lives natively wherever you are. Adding Tryps is dead simple — you add the Tryps contact to the group chat you want to take a trip with. That's it. Now Tryps, the agent, is listening to everything you text in the group chat and actively planning your trip in the background.

---

## The Three Layers

### Layer 1: iMessage (where most people interact)

Most people will interact with Tryps in iMessage. They don't have to do anything different. This is how they're already planning trips today — just with very little organization. Tryps brings structure to the conversation they're already having.

### Layer 2: Mobile App (for people who want to see what's happening)

Tryps has a clean mobile front end for people who really want to see the plan taking shape:

- A clean itinerary section where you can vote on activities
- See when people are landing and who's involved
- Vote on stays like Airbnbs
- Log all expenses

For hardcore travelers, there are features like logging how many countries they've been to and setting up their Traveler DNA.

### Layer 3: Agentic Layer (AI execution on your behalf)

Built on cutting-edge X402 protocols, this layer enables an agent to do things on the user's behalf — booking, coordinating, executing the plan end-to-end.

From the second you're added to a group chat and join, you're instantly asked what you want this trip to feel like. The trip is planned on your behalf. People who don't care about planning still get their preferences captured, and then everything is executed for them.

---

## The Setup Ramp

We believe trips are better when all things are connected. There's a setup process:

- Credit card information
- Airline accounts (e.g., Delta, because that's how you get there)
- Hotel/Airbnb account connections

We walk users through this in a really simple way. Once everything is connected, this is the easiest and best way to book a group trip.
