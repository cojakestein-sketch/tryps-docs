4. Deep Dive: AI Q&A → Instant Group Polling

No topic suggestion response from Jake today. Using his March 29 product north star brain dump: "Make it dead simple to grill an AI about your trip, then AB test every answer with your group via native polling."

What it is

The core product thesis Jake crystallized last night: two pillars that make Tryps undeniable. Pillar 1 is a conversational AI you can "grill" about your trip — ask anything, get great answers. Pillar 2 is the instant handoff to group polling, where every AI answer becomes a native poll the group votes on. The magic is the seamless transition from asking to polling.

Why it matters at this stage

This is the tightest articulation of Tryps' core loop yet. With April 2 release approaching (4 days), this thesis should be the lens for every feature and bug decision. It also resolves the 60/30/10 traveler split: "I Don't Care-ers" just vote on polls, Casual Planners ask the AI questions, and Ultimate Planners curate which AI answers become polls.

Key takeaways

  • The AI Q&A must feel conversational, not transactional. Users should be able to ask follow-up questions naturally. The current agent-intelligence scope (55/61 SC) covers memory and context — that's the foundation.
  • Every AI answer needs a one-tap "Poll the group" button. This is the conversion point. If it takes more than one tap to turn an AI suggestion into a group poll, the magic breaks. Check if output-backed-screen covers this interaction.
  • "AB test" framing is key. Polls shouldn't just be "yes/no" — they should present 2-3 AI-generated options side by side (e.g., "Beach resort in Tulum" vs "Boutique hotel in Mexico City"). This maps to the Vibe tab's preference mechanism.
  • The iMessage agent is the distribution channel. Non-app users see AI suggestions and vote via iMessage. This makes Asif's scope (34/57) the growth engine, not just a feature.
  • Measure the handoff. Track: questions asked → polls created → votes received → decisions made. This funnel is the product's heartbeat.
