# Consulting Services Agreement

**This Consulting Services Agreement** ("Agreement") is entered into as of ____________, 2026 ("Effective Date") by and between:

**Company:** Tryps, Inc. (or applicable entity name), with principal address at ____________ ("Company")

**Consultant:** Cameron ____________, an individual with address at ____________ ("Consultant")

Together referred to as the "Parties."

---

## 1. Engagement

Company engages Consultant as an independent contractor to provide product leadership consulting services ("Services") as described in this Agreement and the attached Exhibit A (Head of Product — Role Scope). Consultant accepts the engagement on the terms set forth herein.

---

## 2. Term

This Agreement begins on the Effective Date and ends on **May 2, 2026** ("End Date"), unless terminated earlier per Section 8. The Parties may extend this Agreement by mutual written consent.

---

## 3. Scope of Services

Consultant will provide product leadership services including but not limited to:

- Product experience audit and spec review across active scopes
- User onboarding demo sessions (sourcing, running, and documenting)
- Dev team working sessions and alignment
- QA mission brief creation based on user feedback
- Launch readiness coordination
- Feedback pipeline setup and triage

The detailed scope is defined in **Exhibit A** (attached). Consultant will dedicate approximately **10 hours per week** to the Services. Consultant determines their own schedule and methods for completing the work.

---

## 4. Compensation — Milestone-Based

Consultant will be compensated based on the completion of the following milestones. Total maximum compensation under this Agreement is **$10,000 USD**.

### Milestone 1: App Store Launch Readiness — $1,500

**Due:** April 15, 2026

**Completion criteria:**
- [ ] Consultant has completed a full personal product walkthrough (iMessage + app + booking flow)
- [ ] Written "new eyes" assessment delivered: first impressions, friction log, top 10 issues
- [ ] Launch readiness checklist delivered covering App Store submission requirements (metadata, screenshots, compliance)
- [ ] Top blocking issues flagged to dev team with severity ranking

### Milestone 2: Launch-Ready Product Signoff — $2,000

**Due:** May 2, 2026

**Completion criteria:**
- [ ] End-to-end flow verified working: new user joins trip via iMessage, opens app, searches flights, books, splits expenses
- [ ] Top 5 blocking issues documented; at least 3 resolved in coordination with dev team
- [ ] Consultant has signed off on launch readiness (written confirmation that the core flow works for real users)

### Milestone 3: 10 Group Trip Demo Sessions — $3,000

**Due:** May 2, 2026

**Completion criteria:**
- [ ] Consultant has sourced and run 10 live demo sessions (20 minutes each, screen share or in person)
- [ ] Each session involves at least 4-5 participants using the product in a real group trip planning capacity
- [ ] At least 3 sessions involve participants actively searching for or booking flights with real payment
- [ ] Structured session log delivered for each session per the template in Exhibit A
- [ ] Synthesized friction report with ranked issues, frequency counts, and severity scores

### Milestone 4: Dev Alignment + QA Pipeline — $2,000

**Due:** May 2, 2026

**Completion criteria:**
- [ ] Attended and contributed to at least 4 dev working sessions during the engagement
- [ ] Weekly dev session cadence established and documented
- [ ] At least 2 QA mission briefs written and delivered to QA team, based on real user feedback from demo sessions
- [ ] "State of the Product" report delivered: ranked friction points, user quotes, NPS data, and recommended product changes

### Milestone 5: Spec Audit + Onboarding Playbook — $1,500

**Due:** May 2, 2026

**Completion criteria:**
- [ ] All 9 active scope specs reviewed with Consultant's written annotations (which success criteria matter for first 50 users vs. which can wait)
- [ ] Written onboarding playbook delivered: a document that enables someone else to run demo sessions without Consultant present
- [ ] Feedback pipeline documented: how feedback comes in, how it gets triaged, where it goes, SLA for processing

### Payment Terms

- Milestone completion is assessed by Company on or before the End Date
- Payment for all completed milestones is due on **May 2, 2026** via bank transfer, check, or mutually agreed method
- Each milestone is independently assessed. Partial completion of the overall engagement does not forfeit payment for fully completed milestones.
- If a milestone is partially complete (e.g., 7 of 10 demo sessions run), Company may, at its sole discretion, pay a prorated amount for that milestone.
- If a milestone is unmet due to factors outside Consultant's reasonable control (e.g., dev team unavailability, API delays, App Store review timing), Company will consider the work completed in good faith.

---

## 5. Independent Contractor Status

Consultant is an independent contractor and not an employee, agent, partner, or joint venturer of Company. Consultant:

- Is responsible for their own taxes, including self-employment tax
- Is not entitled to employee benefits (health insurance, retirement, PTO, etc.)
- Determines their own work schedule and methods
- May provide services to other clients during the term of this Agreement

Company will issue a Form 1099-NEC to Consultant for payments exceeding the applicable IRS reporting threshold.

---

## 6. Intellectual Property Assignment

### 6.1 Work Product

All work product created by Consultant in connection with the Services ("Work Product") — including but not limited to documents, reports, playbooks, specs, analyses, frameworks, session logs, and any other deliverables — is the sole and exclusive property of Company.

### 6.2 Assignment

Consultant hereby irrevocably assigns to Company all right, title, and interest in and to the Work Product, including all intellectual property rights therein. Consultant will execute any documents reasonably requested by Company to perfect this assignment.

### 6.3 Prior Inventions

Any pre-existing intellectual property of Consultant that is incorporated into the Work Product will be identified in writing by Consultant prior to incorporation. Company is granted a perpetual, irrevocable, royalty-free license to use any such prior inventions incorporated into Work Product.

---

## 7. Confidentiality

### 7.1 Confidential Information

During the engagement, Consultant will have access to confidential information including but not limited to: product plans, user data, financial information, strategic plans, codebase, agent architecture, and team communications ("Confidential Information").

### 7.2 Obligations

Consultant agrees to:
- Hold all Confidential Information in strict confidence
- Not disclose Confidential Information to any third party without prior written consent of Company
- Use Confidential Information solely for the purpose of performing the Services
- Return or destroy all Confidential Information upon termination of this Agreement

### 7.3 Survival

Confidentiality obligations survive termination of this Agreement for a period of **two (2) years**.

---

## 8. Termination

Either Party may terminate this Agreement with **seven (7) days' written notice** (email is sufficient).

Upon termination:
- Consultant is paid for all fully completed milestones as of the termination date
- For the milestone in progress at termination, Company will pay a prorated amount reflecting work completed (based on deliverables produced, not hours logged)
- Consultant returns or destroys all Confidential Information and Company property
- IP assignment (Section 6) and confidentiality (Section 7) obligations survive termination

---

## 9. Non-Solicitation

During the term of this Agreement and for **twelve (12) months** following termination, Consultant will not directly or indirectly solicit, recruit, or hire any employee or contractor of Company for employment or engagement elsewhere.

---

## 10. No Authority to Bind

Consultant has no authority to enter into contracts, make commitments, or incur obligations on behalf of Company without prior written authorization from Company's founder/CEO.

---

## 11. Relationship to Future Employment

The Parties acknowledge that this engagement is a mutual evaluation period. Upon successful completion of the milestones and mutual agreement, Company may extend an offer of full-time employment to Consultant.

**Neither Party is obligated to proceed with full-time conversion.** The decision to convert will be made in good faith, based on mutual fit and the successful completion of the milestones described herein. Both Parties will communicate their conversion decision within **fourteen (14) days** of the End Date.

Any full-time employment offer — including title, salary, equity, and benefits — will be set forth in a separate employment agreement, subject to standard terms and board approval. This Agreement creates no right to future employment or equity.

---

## 12. Equity

No equity, stock options, or other ownership interest in Company is granted under this Agreement. Any equity grant will be made solely in connection with a full-time employment offer, if one is extended, under separate documentation and subject to board approval and standard vesting terms.
