## What

Searching, sourcing, and booking flights, stays, activities, restaurants, and transport. The logistics layer — distinct from the trip container (#2) and the decision-making layer (#3).

## Why

Booking is the end of the funnel (vibe → needs → facilitation → booking). Most of the search and display functionality is already built. What remains is restaurant sourcing and Duffel flight booking.

## Success Looks Like

- All existing booking flows QA validated (flights, accommodations, activities, transport, packing, itinerary)
- Restaurant sourcing (Resy, OpenTable) working
- Duffel flight booking integrated
