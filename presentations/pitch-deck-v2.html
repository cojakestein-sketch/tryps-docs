Why Now

Three forces converging for the first time.

Technology

LLMs make conversational agents real

For the first time, an AI agent can understand group conversation, extract intent, run natural polls, and execute bookings. This was impossible 18 months ago.

Infrastructure

iMessage APIs newly accessible

LINQ ($28M raised, Feb 2026) cracked blue-bubble iMessage infrastructure at 90% lower cost than Twilio. Poke went viral on their pipes. We're building on the same rails.

Market

Group travel is the fastest-growing segment

Post-COVID travel boom. Group trips growing 3x faster than solo. 35-40% of leisure travel is group. Gen Z plans everything in group chats. No tool serves them.

The insight LINQ proved: "Every team building an AI agent had the same problem — they couldn't get users to download another app." — Elliott Potter, LINQ CEO
