# Tryps Creator Program

**Launch Date:** May 2, 2026
**Program:** 80 creators. 120 pieces of content. 3 weeks.
**Budget:** $8,000

---

## What Tryps Is

Tryps is a travel agent that lives in your iMessage group chat. You add the Tryps contact to a group chat with your friends, and it plans your entire trip through the conversation — destinations, flights, hotels, itineraries, cost splitting. No app to download separately. It works where your friend group already talks.

**Core audience:** 22-28 year old post-grads who travel in groups. The person in every friend group who actually makes plans happen.

---

## The Creator Program

We're activating 80 creators across TikTok and Instagram to make Tryps feel like it's everywhere during launch.

**Two tiers:**

- **10-15 mid-tier creators** (50K-500K followers) — credibility names that make the brand feel legitimate
- **65-70 micro creators** (10K-50K followers) — the volume layer that creates the wave

**Three phases over 3 weeks:**

### Phase 1: Day 1 Blitz (May 2)

All 80 creators repost the Tryps giveaway contest on their Stories within 24 hours of launch. The prize: Tryps pays for the winner's dream trip, destination of their choice. 80 Stories hitting simultaneously spikes the algorithm and creates the impression that Tryps is everywhere overnight.

### Phase 2: Paid Original Content (May 10-16)

20 creators post original videos. The hook: "Finally I don't have to book my own trips anymore" or "Why I prefer planning my trips on Tryps." The creator gives a brief explanation of how they use it, filmed by a friend while the creator shows the process on their phone — not a screen recording or screenshot, but someone physically holding their phone and walking through it on camera. It feels natural because it looks like a friend filming a friend, not an ad.

### Phase 3: Paid Original Content (May 17-23)

20 more creators post original videos. Same format, different creators, different destinations. Three consecutive weeks of creators organically talking about Tryps keeps the product in people's feeds long after launch day.
