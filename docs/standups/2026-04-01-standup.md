### Mission 2: Feedback Mechanism
How do we collect, route, and act on user feedback? Three channels:
- **Shake-to-feedback:** Instabug or similar — shake the phone, attach screenshot, submit.
- **Text keyword:** Users text `feedback:` followed by anything to the Tryps agent. The agent recognizes this keyword and routes it to a feedback inbox.
- **Agent-prompted:** Tryps proactively tells users: "We're in beta. Text 'feedback:' followed by anything and it goes straight to our team."
- **Triage:** All feedback — shake, text, agent-collected — lands in one place for Jake to review daily.
