# How Tryps Works

## Layer 1: iMessage — Where most people interact

Add the Tryps contact to your group chat. That's it. Tryps listens to everything you text and **actively plans your trip in the background**. No new app required for most of the group.

## Layer 2: Mobile App — For people who want to see the plan

- Clean itinerary with voting on activities
- See when everyone's landing and who's involved
- Vote on stays, log expenses, track the group

## Layer 3: Agent Layer — AI that executes on your behalf

Built on **X402 protocols** — our agent can search, book, and coordinate on behalf of the group. From the second you join, you're asked what you want this trip to feel like. Then it's planned *for* you.

# The Setup Ramp

Trips are better when everything is connected. There's a simple setup:

- **Credit card** — for seamless group payments
- **Airline accounts** — Delta, United, etc. (that's how you get there)
- **Hotel / Airbnb** — account connections for booking

Once connected, this is the **easiest and best way to book a group trip**. The agent has everything it needs to act on your behalf.
