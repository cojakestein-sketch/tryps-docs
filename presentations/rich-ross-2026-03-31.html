The Problem

Trip planning happens in iMessage. Nothing acts on it.

Plans get buried in 200+ messages
Dates, prices, preferences — scattered and lost
One person becomes the unpaid travel agent
6+ apps, zero integration, all on one person
Trips die in the group chat
"We should totally go" never becomes "we're going"
The Solution

An agent in your iMessage that does it all.

1 Captures
Reads intent from natural conversation
2 Facilitates
Runs polls, steers group toward decisions
3 Books
Flights, stays, activities — on the group's behalf
Business Model

In exchange for being where you are, facilitating your trip, and booking on your behalf — we earn a 1% booking fee.

Native app for itinerary, expenses, and trip details. iMessage via Linq for natural language trip creation and group polling.

What the content looks like

"I added Tryps to my group chat and now we're going to Vegas. It was easy."

Organic discovery energy. Filmed by a friend, not a tripod. The creator walks through adding the Tryps contact to their group chat, shows the trip getting planned, and reacts. It should feel like they found something and want to show it off — not like they're reading a brief.
