## Upwork Post: GTM Growth Engineer

**Title:** AI-Native Growth Engineer — Consumer App Launching May 2

**Category:** Marketing > Growth Marketing

**Hours:** 30-40 hrs/week

**Schedule:** Must overlap with US Eastern Time (roughly 9AM-9PM ET, core hours 10AM-6PM ET)

---

**Description:**

We're Tryps — a group travel planning app launching May 2, 2026. You add a travel agent to your iMessage group chat, text it your trip idea, and it plans the whole thing. Think Partiful but for travel.

We're building a small brand and go-to-market team under our creative director. We need a flexible, AI-native growth engineer who can execute across our entire GTM operation — not a specialist in one channel.

**What this role looks like:**

You're the person our creative director and founder point at whatever the GTM org needs that week. One week you're writing App Store copy and running keyword tests. The next you're managing Meta ad spend and flagging which UGC clips are winning. The week after that you're writing SEO landing pages, prepping our Product Hunt launch, or building a press kit.

You live in the terminal. You use Claude Code or Cursor daily — not as a novelty, but as your primary way of getting work done. You're technical enough to write scripts, build workflows, and ship landing pages. You're marketing-savvy enough to write copy that converts and know what good content looks like. You're fast enough to produce volume. The goal is 100+ distributed content pieces per month across social, ads, search, App Store, and email.

**Week-to-week scope includes:**

- Social content production — screen recordings, carousels, clip edits using templates and AI tools. Schedule and publish across 8 platforms.
- Paid social — manage Meta ad spend, generate ad variants from UGC creator content, A/B test, optimize. Experience with Meta Ads Manager or posting content on social platforms is helpful.
- App Store optimization — write and test metadata, run Apple PPO screenshot tests, monitor conversion rates.
- SEO / GEO / AEO — write landing pages and blog posts targeting high-intent keywords. Implement structured data so AI search engines (ChatGPT, Perplexity, Claude) recommend Tryps.
- UGC operational support — run creators through verification tools, track deliverables, manage payment logistics. (Our creative director owns the relationships.)
- Email — write launch blast and onboarding drip sequences.
- Product Hunt — prep listing and coordinate launch day.
- Press support — build journalist list, draft pitches, create press kit.
- Whatever else needs doing that week.

**You are NOT:**

- A strategist. The strategy is set. We need execution at speed.
- A specialist. If you can only do one thing (just SEO, just Meta ads), this isn't the right fit.
- A designer. You use AI tools and templates, not Figma from scratch.
- Managing influencer relationships. Our creative director handles that.

**What we're looking for:**

- You work in the terminal daily — Claude Code, Cursor, or similar AI-native dev tools are part of your actual workflow, not something you've "tried."
- You use AI tools (Claude, ChatGPT, Midjourney, CapCut, Canva, etc.) as a multiplier and can point to specific workflows where AI made you 5-10x faster.
- You've worked with social content, posting, or ad platforms before. Deep Meta Ads Manager expertise isn't required, but comfort with paid social and content distribution is.
- You can write — copy, emails, landing pages, App Store descriptions. Not novelist-level, but clear and persuasive.
- You can build — scripts, simple web pages, automation workflows. When something doesn't exist, you make it.
- When asked to do something you've never done, your answer is "give me 2 hours" not "that's not my job."
- Startup speed. We ship fast, iterate faster.

**Nice to have:**

- App Store optimization (ASO) experience
- TikTok Spark Ads experience
- SEO / GEO / content marketing background
- Previous consumer app launch experience
- n8n, Make, or similar automation tool experience
- Remotion or programmatic video generation

**Logistics:**

- 30-40 hours/week contractor
- Must overlap with US Eastern Time (core hours 10AM-6PM ET)
- Slack access, 2-3x weekly meetings with creative director and founder
- Start immediately — we launch May 2

2. Describe your daily AI development setup. What's in your terminal? Do you use Claude Code, Cursor, or similar? Show me a screenshot or describe your workflow. We need someone who lives in these tools, not someone who's "interested in AI."
