## Job Description

We're building **Tryps**, a collaborative group travel planning app where an AI agent lives in your iMessage group chat and handles the logistics — flights, hotels, restaurants, expenses, and more. Think: the future of trip planning, powered by autonomous agents and real human coordination.

We're a small, fast-moving team (5 people) shipping a 19-scope product across 5 phases — and we need one more engineer who can hit the ground running.

### What You'll Be Working On

Our pipeline spans ~247 screens and includes:

- **Core App (Phase 1):** Finishing the remaining 53% of 19 major user flows — trip creation, itinerary management, expense tracking, notifications & voting infrastructure, post-trip time capsule with AI-generated video montages, and Travel DNA profiling
- **Integration Layer (Phase 2):** Stripe payment processing for group bookings, iMessage agent via Linq, travel document storage (passport/loyalty), and booking link embeds
- **Agent Layer (Phase 3):** Autonomous logistics agent that searches flights/hotels/restaurants via Duffel & Amadeus APIs, group decision-making with voting windows, preference-based auto-voting, and **x402 micropayment protocol** for agent spending
- **Bug fixes & production stability:** We have 137K lines of code, 1,379+ passing tests, and a live app that needs ongoing maintenance, bug triage, and deployment support

### The Ideal Candidate

You are someone who:

- Has **4-5 years of React Native experience** shipping production mobile apps (iOS and Android)
- Has **2-3 years of hands-on Supabase experience** — you know Row-Level Security, real-time subscriptions, edge functions, and auth flows inside and out
- Has strong **PostgreSQL database experience** — schema design, migrations, query optimization, and data modeling
- Has **expert-level proficiency with Claude Code, Codex, or equivalent AI-assisted development tools** — this is how we build. Our entire pipeline is agent-driven. If you haven't used these tools daily, this isn't the role
- **Understands engineering at the agentic layer** — MCP servers, tool-calling patterns, autonomous agent loops, and ideally has worked with **x402 (HTTP 402 Payment Required)** micropayment protocols
- Has experience **deploying and maintaining production applications** — you've pushed builds, triaged production bugs, managed app store submissions, and kept real users happy
- Writes **TypeScript** fluently with strict typing
- Is comfortable with **Expo** (push notifications, builds, OTA updates)
- Can work within an existing **137K-line codebase** without needing to rewrite everything — you read code well and make surgical, tested changes

### Bonus Points

- Experience with Stripe Connect or Stripe payment integrations
- Familiarity with Duffel, Amadeus, or travel booking APIs
- Experience with video processing (FFmpeg) or media pipelines
- Has shipped apps that handle real-time collaboration or group features
- Experience with Maestro or similar E2E mobile testing frameworks
- Understanding of OAuth 2.1 PKCE flows
