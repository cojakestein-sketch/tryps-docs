# Tryps Physical Presence / Street Marketing Plan

**Launch:** May 2, 2026 | **Budget:** $2-3K | **Location:** NYC

---

## 1. Wheat-Paste Posters

### Design
- Tryps Red (#D9071C) background, white text
- Primary: "Text Marty. He'll plan the whole trip." + QR code
- Secondary: "Stop letting Josh plan the trip." + QR code
- Tertiary: "27 texts about where to eat in Barcelona. Or just text Marty."

### Where (NYC)
**Tier 1 (60% of budget):** Williamsburg/Bushwick/Greenpoint
- Bedford Ave (N 4th to N 12th), Grand St, Wythe Ave near hotels, Bushwick near House of Yes/Elsewhere

**Tier 2:** LES/East Village/Nolita
- Rivington/Orchard, St. Marks, Mulberry/Elizabeth

**Tier 3:** Astoria, West Village, Union Square/NYU

### Cost
- Print 150 posters at 24x36: $300
- Wheat paste + rollers: $25
- Freelance paste crew (80 spots): $500
- **Total: $825**

### Timeline
- April 14: Finalize designs, send to print
- April 21-25: First wave pasting
- April 28-May 1: Second wave
- May 2: Launch day, posters have been up 10+ days

## 2. Stickers

### Designs
1. **Logo die-cut** — Tryps logo on white, for laptops/bottles
2. **"text marty."** — White bg, red text, 4x2 rectangle
3. **iMessage bubble** — "where are we going this summer?" with "Marty knows." below
4. **"GO WHILE YOU CAN"** — Red circle, white text, feels like a travel patch

### Distribution
- Coffee shops in Williamsburg, Greenpoint, LES, East Village
- Coworking spaces
- Hostels (HI NYC, The Local, NY Moore)
- College campuses (NYU, The New School, Pratt)

### Cost
- 1,300 stickers from StickerMule: ~$300
- Order by April 14, arrive by April 18

## 3. Branded Film Cameras

### Concept
Tryps-branded disposable cameras go to friend groups. They shoot on trips, share photos tagging @jointryps.

### Sourcing
- 40 Fujifilm QuickSnaps from B&H Photo (NYC): $520
- Custom vinyl wraps/stickers: $150
- **Total: $670**

### Distribution
- Launch event: 15 cameras
- Influencer/creator gifting: 15 cameras
- College campus drops: 10 cameras

## 4. Extra Ideas

- **Luggage tags** ($150 for 200): Tryps Red, logo + QR code. Useful, travels with the person.
- **Bar coasters** ($100 for 500): "Marty says try the mezcal." Drop at Williamsburg bars.
- **Sidewalk chalk stencils** ($30): "Text Marty" + QR near NYU and Bedford Ave.
- **Bodega pop-up** ($500): Partner with one Williamsburg coffee shop for launch week. Tryps-branded coffee sleeves, countertop display.
