# Traveler DNA & Trip Vibe

## What Makes Tryps Unique

We are actually capturing *you* as the traveler. When you join a trip, one of the first things we ask is that you answer what's called a **Trip Vibe** — what do you want out of this trip?

### Example Trip Vibe Questions (A/B style)

- Do you want to go chill, or do you want to party?
- Do you want to be packed, or do you want to take it easy?
- Do you want to wake up early, or stay up late?

These are quick A/B-type questions designed to capture preferences fast.

## How It Works

For each user in the trip, we gather their Trip Vibe and bring it all together. This lets us provide best-in-class recommendations for what we believe will make the trip best for *all* people in the group.
