# Tryps Giveaway Campaign Concepts

**Budget:** ~$5-8K | **Launch:** May 2, 2026

---

## Concept 1: "Marty Plans Your Dream Trip"
*The flagship. One winning group gets a full trip planned and paid for by Marty.*

### Hook
"Your group chat talks about trips that never happen. Marty's about to fix that."

### Prize
$5,000 trip for a group of up to 6 friends, fully planned by Marty. Flights, hotel, activities. Winners pick the vibe, Marty handles the rest. The trip becomes content for Tryps (with permission).

### Mechanics
1. Download Tryps and add Marty to your group chat
2. Text Marty your dream trip (destination, vibe, crew size)
3. Screenshot the conversation and post to IG Story or TikTok tagging @jointryps with #MartyPlanMyTrip
4. Every person in the group chat who also posts = bonus entry for the whole group

Entry unit is the GROUP, not the individual. One person enters, then pressures friends to also post for better odds.

### Virality Loop
- One person downloads and enters, tells 3-5 friends to post too
- Every entry is a public post showing a real Marty conversation (product demo disguised as giveaway entry)
- People see the posts, go "wait what is this," download to try Marty themselves
- Winning group's trip gets documented and posted, creating a second wave

### Timeline
- April 14: Announce across all platforms
- April 14-28: Entry window (2 weeks)
- April 28: Entries close
- May 2 (launch day): Winner announced live

### Cost
- Trip prize: $5,000
- Legal review of official rules: $300-500
- Total: ~$5,300-5,500

## Concept 2: "The Group Chat Race"
*Competitive, time-boxed. Friend groups compete against each other.*

### Hook
"The first group chat to fill a trip wins it for free."

### Prize
$2,500 trip for winning group (up to 5 friends).

### Mechanics
1. Download Tryps, add Marty to group chat
2. Text Marty a trip idea
3. Every person in the group who downloads Tryps = 1 point
4. Group with most verified downloads by deadline wins
5. Bonus: post Marty conversation with #GroupChatRace for 5 extra points per post

### Virality Loop
- Every group tries to get more people to download (downloads = points)
- Groups post on social to recruit outside their friend circle
- Daily leaderboard creates FOMO
- Even losing groups have onboarded their whole friend group onto the app

### Timeline
- April 21: Race begins (10-day sprint)
- Daily: Tryps posts leaderboard updates
- May 1: Race ends, winner announced
- May 2: Winner's trip planning begins live

### Cost
- Trip prize: $2,500
- Legal: $300-500
- Total: ~$2,800-3,000

## Concept 3: "Marty's Mystery Trip"
*Nobody knows where they're going. Marty picks. The chaos is the content.*

### Hook
"Tell Marty your budget and your crew. He picks the destination. You don't find out until the airport."

### Prize
$2,000 mystery trip for 4 friends. Destination revealed at the airport.

### Mechanics
1. Download Tryps, add Marty to group chat
2. Text Marty: "Plan us a mystery trip. Budget: [X]. Dates: [X]."
3. Screenshot Marty's cryptic response ("pack for warm weather, that's all you're getting") and post to TikTok with #MartysMysteryTrip
4. Tag your crew, every tagged person who also posts = bonus entry
5. Tryps picks winner based on best content (judged, not random)

### Virality Loop
- Mystery element is inherently shareable
- Marty's cryptic hints become their own meme format
- Airport reveal video gets 500K+ views organically on TikTok
- Post-trip vlog creates third wave of content

### Timeline
- April 18: Campaign launches (10-day window)
- May 2: Winner announced
- May/June: Mystery trip happens, documented in real-time

### Cost
- Trip prize: $2,000
- Legal: $300-500
- Total: ~$2,300-2,500

---

## How to Combine

If budget is $5K: Run Concept 1 only.
If budget is $8-10K: Concept 1 (Dream Trip, $5K) + Concept 2 (Group Chat Race, $2.5K).
Best virality per dollar: Concept 3 (Mystery Trip) generates disproportionate attention.

**Key across all concepts:** Every entry mechanic requires adding Marty to a group chat. The giveaway IS the product demo.
