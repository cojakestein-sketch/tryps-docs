## What

Getting the word out. Landing page update (jointryps.com), App Store listing, launch video (hero + social cuts), social media strategy, Wispr Flow playbook (UGC), referral incentives, and giveaways (Dream Trip).

## Why

Building the product is half the job. Distribution is the other half. The landing page and App Store listing are needed for beta distribution. The rest drives awareness and acquisition post-launch.

## Success Looks Like

- Landing page updated and live at jointryps.com
- App Store listing ready for submission
- Launch video produced (Poke-inspired, Sean owns creative)
- Social media strategy executing (Twitter first, then Instagram, TikTok)
- Referral and giveaway programs designed and launched
