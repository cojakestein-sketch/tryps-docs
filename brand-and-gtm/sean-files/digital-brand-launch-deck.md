## Platform Strategies

### Instagram (@jointryps -- The Storefront)

Instagram is the first thing someone checks after hearing about Tryps. The profile needs to look like a real brand from day one. 9-post grid pre-loaded before launch. Campaign posters, app mockups, and video. Everything is high production, color graded, Tryps red. No filler.

After launch, Instagram becomes the lifestyle platform. Swipe posts, stories (daily polls, BTS, app tips), and reels repurposed from TikTok. Mid-20s to early 30s professionals live here. This is where brand trust is built.

**No competitor has a real Instagram brand.** Splitwise: 6.9K followers, minimal effort. Wanderlog: 26K, sparse posting. TripIt: 11K, corporate. Tryps can be the Partiful of group trips on IG from day one.

**Pre-loaded 9-post grid:**

| | | |
|---|---|---|
| Campaign Poster 1 | App Mockup | Campaign Poster 2 |
| Commercial (pinned) | Campaign Poster 3 | App Mockup |
| Campaign Poster 4 | App Tutorial | Campaign Poster 5 |

**Posting schedule:**
- **Week 1:** 9-post grid live before launch. Commercial pinned. Daily stories. 1 reel. 1 swipe post.
- **Week 2:** 2 feed posts. 1 reel (Jake demo). 1 swipe post. Daily stories.
- **Week 3:** 2 feed posts (social proof + poster). 1 reel. Daily stories. Metrics report.

---

### TikTok (@tryps -- The Discovery Engine)

TikTok is the #1 platform for travel app discovery. 42% of users have made travel purchases from content they saw. The algorithm doesn't care about followers. A day-one account can reach millions if the video is right.

**The format that converts:** Jake on camera, demos the AI travel agent in the group chat, reacts to it working. Different hook every time, same demo. This is the Wispr Flow model (500M views in 60 days with the same format repeated).

**Paid ads on TikTok** amplify the best organic content. $0.50-2.88 CPI. We take whatever video performs best organically and put ad spend behind it. Cheapest path to downloads.

**No competitor is on TikTok.** Splitwise, TripIt, Lambus -- none of them.

**Content format (every video):**
- **0-3 sec:** Hook. Bold claim or question that stops the scroll.
- **3-30 sec:** Demo. Jake texts in the group chat, AI agent responds, trip gets planned in real time.
- **30-45 sec:** Reaction + CTA. "Link in bio." Download and try it.

**Paid ads strategy:**
- Platform: TikTok Ads Manager
- CPI: $0.50-2.88 (cheapest for travel apps)
- Creative: Top organic videos repurposed as ads. Not separate ad creative.
- Targeting: 18-34, travel interests, competitor app users
- Optimization: Daily. Shift budget to lowest CPI creative.

**Posting schedule:**
- **Week 1:** Daily posting. Launch video Day 1, Commercial Day 2, Tutorial Day 3, Jake demos Days 4-7. Paid ads go live Day 1.
- **Week 2:** 3 Jake demo videos (bachelor party, family reunion, friend group weekend). Ads optimizing daily.
- **Week 3:** 2-3 Jake demos + 1 remixed top performer with new hook. Final metrics.

### X (Jake Stein's Personal -- Founder Presence)

X is not the download driver. TikTok and Apple Search Ads do that. X is where the tech/startup world talks. Jake's personal account is the channel. Not a brand account.

**The first 30-60 minutes on X decide everything.** Engagement velocity (comments, retweets, quote tweets in the first hour) determines whether the algorithm pushes the post to thousands or millions. That's why we activate 20-50 people to engage immediately on launch day.

**Jake replies to EVERY comment for 48 hours.** Replies create new reply chains, which is the #1 ranking signal on X. This is non-negotiable. Sean pre-writes all the posts. Jake just has to show up and engage.

**Launch day plan:**
- **8:00 AM:** Hero video drops on Jake's personal X
- **8:10 AM:** Activate network: text 20-50 people to like, comment, retweet NOW
- **8-10 AM:** Critical window. Jake replies to every single comment.
- **ALL DAY:** Keep replying. Share reactions. Post "Why I Built Tryps" thread in the afternoon.
- **48 HRS:** Jake stays active. Replies to everything. Non-negotiable.

**Posting schedule:**
- **Pre-launch:** 2-3 posts/week. Teaser content, behind-the-scenes.
- **Week 1:** Launch day hero post + "Why I Built Tryps" thread. 1 post/day. Build-in-public updates.
- **Week 2:** 2-3 posts. Milestone updates. Quote tweet anyone talking about Tryps.
- **Week 3:** 2 posts. Milestone update + genuine reflection.

### LinkedIn (Jake Stein -- Professional Credibility)

LinkedIn is not a volume play. 1x per week, quality only. Every post has a purpose: establish Jake as a credible founder building something real. Investors, journalists, and potential partners check LinkedIn before taking a meeting.

**Post format:** Long-form text with a personal angle. Not promotional. "Here's what I learned," "here's what surprised me," "here's what I built and why."

Sean writes the posts. Jake reviews and publishes from his personal account.

**Why LinkedIn matters:**
- **Press:** Journalists check founders' LinkedIn before writing about them.
- **Investors:** Build-in-public posts signal traction.
- **Partnerships:** Travel brands, airlines, hotels -- partnership leads come from LinkedIn.
- **Hiring:** Visible founder with a launch story attracts talent.

**Posting schedule:**
- **Pre-launch:** 1 post teasing the launch.
- **Week 1:** Long-form "Why I Built Tryps" story + milestone update.
- **Week 2:** 1 post: surprises, learnings, or user story.
- **Week 3:** 1 post: reflection + repost any press coverage.
